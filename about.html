Our story

we wished existed.

WriteClean started from a simple observation: the most popular grammar tool in the world had thousands of angry Trustpilot reviews about surprise charges, auto-renewals, and writing that came out sounding robotic.

We read them all. Then we built WriteClean to fix every single one.

What we believe about free tools

🔓
Free means free
Not "free tier." Not "free trial." Not "free with credit card." WriteClean is free every time you use it, forever. No upgrade wall. No word limit. No email required.
🎭
Your voice matters
Grammar correction should fix errors, not replace the writer. Voice-Preserving Mode was built from day one — it's not a premium feature, it's a default.

Privacy by default
We don't store your text. We don't have an account for you because we don't need one. Your writing stays yours, processed and forgotten.

No dark patterns
No pre-checked annual subscription boxes. No "free trial" that converts to billing. No buttons designed to confuse you into paying. WriteClean has no billing system at all.

Accessible to everyone
Students, ESL writers, freelancers in lower-income markets — good grammar tools should not cost $144/year. They should be free. Always.
